Gradient flow

Willmore flow (Willmore能量的L2梯度流). Energy functional \begin{align*} E( \Gamma) = \frac12 \int_ \Gamma H^2 \end{align*}

应用：

  • image processing

  • modeling of biological membranes

Considering an evolving surface \(\Gamma(t)\) with normal velocity field \(v = Vn\). Then \begin{align*} \delta E[ \Gamma](V) = \dfrac{d}{dt}|_{t=0} E( \Gamma) &= \dfrac{d}{dt} (\frac12 \int_ \Gamma H^2) = \int_ \Gamma H { \partial_t^\bullet} H + \frac12 H^2 \nabla_ \Gamma\cdot v\\ &= \int_ \Gamma H { \partial_t^\bullet} H + \frac12 H^3 V\\ &= \int_ \Gamma (- \Delta_ \Gamma H -|A|^2 H + \frac12 H^3)V \end{align*} where the last line is from integration by part（分部积分） and the evolution equation（演化方程） \begin{align*} { \partial_t^\bullet} H = - \Delta_ \Gamma V - |A|^2 V \end{align*} Now we seek the gradient of the functional, which is the Riesz representative of the Frechet’s derivative \begin{align*} ( \dfrac{ \delta E }{ \delta V}, V)_H = \delta E[ \Gamma](V). \end{align*}

\(L^2\) gradient flow – Willmore flow

Taking \(H = L^2( \Gamma)\), then the gradient flow is \begin{align*} V = -\dfrac{ \delta E }{ \delta V} = \Delta_ \Gamma H + |A|^2 H - \frac12 H^3 \end{align*} This is the Willmore flow.

Harmonic map heat flow

  • Star流；对所有的\(v\)满足 \(v\cdot u =0\)的限制，求解满足 \(\partial_t u\cdot u = 0\)以及 \begin{align*} (\partial_t u, v)_{\star} + (\nabla u, \nabla v) = 0 \end{align*} 的解

  • 如果时间导数选取的是H1范数，则相当于是 \begin{align*} \partial_t u = (\Delta)^{-1} \Delta u \end{align*} 第一个\((\Delta)^{-1}\)对应的正是\(H^1\)梯度流

  • 相反的，如果是\(H^{-1}\)梯度流，正如surface diffusion那样，会额外多出Laplacian。因此，现在空间算子具有更大的domain，让它更加可能接近RK的最优阶

\begin{align*} h^2 \int_F d_t e \nabla d_t e \cdot n &\lesssim h^2 ( \epsilon^{-1} h^{-1}\| d_t e \|_{F}^2 + \epsilon h \|\nabla d_t e\|_{F}^2 )\\ &\lesssim \epsilon^{-1} h\| d_t e \|_{F}^2 + \epsilon h^2 \|\nabla d_t e\|_{T}^2 \end{align*} 第二项被分部积分的正项吸收。第一项可以用迹不等式 \begin{align*} \lesssim \epsilon^{-1} \| d_t e \|_{T}^2 \end{align*}
